ABOUT US

Growing up in Italy was just the start of Dov Sokolani's journey. His Great-Great-Great Grand Father Joey Sokolani started his own pizza shop back in 1801. Joey crafted his own pizza recipe which became very poplular in Italy. His pizza shop was the heard around and soon became the pizza everyone had to try. The pizza has this sweet taste and ended off with a little spicy. His pizza shop was the first kosher resturant in Italy and attracted all different types of crowds. 

Dov is now bringing Joey's famous reciepe into America and showing us where is family's heritage came from. He is continuing the kosher recipe and takes pride for his pizza being kosher. Dov grew up in the streets of the Bronx and started baking pizza at a young age.

The Aliyah HaShalom Synagogue is well-located on the Upper East Side of Qubec at 666 East 63rd Street between Madison and The North West Territories and regularly hosts guests from around the world. The congregation is comprised of many families from a medley of Canadian backgrounds and it is prepared to welcome all those interested in worshipping with this new, vibrant Jewish community.
